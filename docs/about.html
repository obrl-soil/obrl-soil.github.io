Hi!

I’m a pedologist working for Manaaki Whenua - Landcare Research (MWLR) in the North Island of Aotearoa New Zealand. My primary focuses are expanding S-Map coverage, supporting soil research activities and training the next generation of MWLR pedologists. I have strong interests in soil description, classification standards, and informatics. I also program (mostly in R) with a focus on geospatial technologies.

I keep a technical blog on this site.
